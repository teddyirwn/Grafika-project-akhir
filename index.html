📜 Match Rules:

  • Each player starts with 100% Health Bar.
  • Each attack (C) deals 20% damage to the enemy.
  • First player to reach 0% health loses the round.
  • Match is Best of 3 Rounds — win 2 rounds to win the match.
  • Each round has a 60-second timer. If time runs out, the player with more health wins the round.
  • Winner earns +10 points, loser loses -5 points.
  • If your opponent disconnects, you win the match automatically.
  • Use jump (W) to dodge your opponent's attacks!
